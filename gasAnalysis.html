Description

This is a analysing tool for gas prices in the United State. Thare 6 main functionalities to use. It visualizes the gas prices in different plots and shows the statistics of the data. Also, It can predict the feture gas prices with a simple mechine learning tool.

What I did

  • Developed a software to analyze gas prices in the U.S. with Python.
  • Collected over 100,000 gas price data from 28 states through web scraping, and organized and retrieved the data with SQL to a DB file.
  • Utilized GUI(Tkinter) and Matplotlib to visualize the gas prices with different statistics
  • Predicted future gas prices with simple Tensorflow.
