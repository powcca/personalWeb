Description

Gitlet is a version-control system that mimics some of the basic features of the popular system Git. The main functionality are Saving the contents of entire directories of files, Restoring a version of one or more files or entire commits, Viewing the history of your backups, Maintaining related sequences of commits, Merging changes made in one branch into another.

What I did

  • Developed a git-like version-control system with Java.
  • Provided various commands such as add, commit, push, branch, checkout, merge, etc.
  • Designed effecient algorithms to solve the commited records tracking problem.
